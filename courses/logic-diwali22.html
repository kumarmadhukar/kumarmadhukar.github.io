Evaluation and Assessment

  • Assignments: There will be 6 assignments in the course, of 7 marks each. Delayed submission will cost you one mark for the first day of delay, and then 2 marks for every subsequent day of delay.

  • Quizzes: There will be 8 surprize quizzes in the class (at any time during the class), of 3 marks each. There cannot be any make-up surprize quizzes, of course, but I will count the best 6.

  • Minor exam: 15 marks

  • Major exam: 25 marks

  • Pass: In order to pass, you would need 31 marks or more, and attendance in at least 2 quizzes.

  • Audit: You will be awarded an audit pass if you have submitted at least 3 assignments, participated in at least 4 quizzes, and have obtained at least 51 marks in all.

  • There will be zero tolerance for dishonest means like copying solutions from others, and even letting others copy your solution, in any quiz/exam/assignment. If you are found indulging in such an activity, you will get no marks in that quiz/exam/assignment. Second-time offenders will be summarily awarded an F grade.
