Evaluation and Assessment

  • Quizzes (40 marks): There will be 5 surprize quizzes in the class (at any time during the class), of 10 marks each. We will count the best 4 out of 5. If you miss a quiz, we will assign you the marks for that quiz by appropriately scaling your marks in the next quiz/exam that you attend. But this scaling will be done only if you have at least 75 percent attendance. If your attendance is below 75 percent, you will be given zero marks for quizzes that you have missed.

  • Minor exam (25 marks): If you miss the minor exam, we will scale your major exam marks to calculate your marks for the minor. There won't be a re-minor.

  • Major exam (35 marks): The syllabus for the major exam will include everything that is covered in the entire course. A re-major exam will be held, if necessary.

  • Grading: We will adopt a relative grading scheme.

  • Pass and Audit Pass criteria: You need to obtain 30 marks or more in order to pass the course. For an audit pass, you need at least 75 percent attendance and at least 60 marks in the course.

  • There will be zero tolerance for dishonest means like copying solutions from others, and even letting others copy your solution, in any quiz/exam/. If you are found indulging in such an activity, your answer paper will not be evaluated and your participation/submission will not be counted. Second-time offenders will be summarily awarded an F grade.
